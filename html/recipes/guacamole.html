Guacamole

About:

Guacamole is a mexican dish with avocado as primary ingredient. It is a very tasty and hearty dish popular in North America, particularly in US where it is often referred to as "Guac". Most commonly guacamole is eaten as dip for nacho chips, but can also be served as a salad.

Ingredients:

  • 2 avocados peeled and pitted
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilatro
  • 2 table spoons fresh lime or lemon juice
  • 1 jalapeno pepper seeded and minced
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Mash avocados in a bowl until creamy.
  2. Mix avocado mash with all other ingredients well. Season with salt and black pepper.
